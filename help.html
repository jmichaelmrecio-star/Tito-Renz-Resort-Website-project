QReserve System Guides

Find step-by-step instructions for using our web-based reservation system.

Guide 1: How to Book a Reservation

  1. Click the **Reserve Now** link in the navigation bar.
  2. Select your desired service type (Room or Cottage).
  3. Enter the required **Check-in** and **Check-out** dates and times.
  4. Fill in the number of guests.
  5. Click **Confirm & Submit Reservation** to proceed to payment simulation.

Guide 2: QR Code Check-in Process

  1. After confirming your reservation, you will be shown a unique **QR Code**.
  2. Save this page or take a screenshot of the QR code.
  3. Upon arrival at the resort, present the code to the staff member.
  4. The staff will scan the code using the QReserve system to instantly verify your booking details and process your **check-in** seamlessly.

Guide 3: Viewing Past Bookings

Registered users can view their current and past reservations.

  1. Ensure you are logged into your account.
  2. Click on the **My Profile** link in the navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down to the **My Past Reservations** section to view your booking history and status.
